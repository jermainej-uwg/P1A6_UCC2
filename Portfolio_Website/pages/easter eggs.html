Notable Easter Eggs

A rotating list of memorable secrets and where to find them.

Hidden Room: The Old Theater

Access: perform the midnight sequence at the central plaza, then interact with the broken marquee. Reward: unique cosmetic and a short developer message.

Secret Vendor: Midnight Market

Trigger: complete the Lost Signal side task and return to the docks after 22:00. The vendor sells rare crafting materials and a one-off lore entry.

Secret Quests

Step-by-step notes for unlocking hidden quests and rewards.

Secret Quest: The Lost Signal

Overview: a multi-step stealth and puzzle sequence that unlocks a hidden vendor and a lore entry.

  1. Visit the abandoned radio tower at night.
  2. Collect the signal key from the derelict lab.
  3. Interact with the antenna after restoring power.
