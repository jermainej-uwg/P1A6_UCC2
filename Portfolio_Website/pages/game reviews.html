Featured Game Reviews

Two recent full reviews with verdicts and platform-specific notes.

Echoes of the Foundry — 8.5/10

Verdict: strong worldbuilding and combat systems, with occasional pacing issues in the midgame. Recommended for players who enjoy exploration and layered encounter design.

Platforms tested: PC, PS5, Xbox Series X. Performance: stable 60fps on Series X with high settings; PC performance varies by GPU—see full review for settings and benchmarks.

Silent Harbor — 6.5/10

Verdict: evocative atmosphere and strong audio design, but mission structure is uneven and some systems feel underdeveloped. Best experienced for the setting rather than tight gameplay loops.

Platforms tested: Switch 2 (handheld mode), PS5. Notes: Switch 2 handheld performance is solid with reduced draw distance; PS5 patch improved load times but not all frame dips.
