Master Collector

Collect every region-specific artifact. Estimated time: 3–5 hours. Tips: follow the region order to avoid backtracking.

Speed Demon

Complete the main story under 4 hours. Recommended: skip optional dialogue, use fast-travel routes, and follow the speedrun routing checklist.

Checklists

Completionist Checklist

  1. Finish main story on any difficulty.
  2. Collect all region artifacts (follow region order).
  3. Complete all faction reputation lines.
  4. Unlock all vendor-exclusive items.
  5. Finish all time-limited events (if available).

Challenge Achievements

  • Defeat the Iron Sentinel without taking damage (practice phase patterns).
  • Complete the gauntlet under 10 minutes (recommended loadout: high mobility).
  • Find and activate all hidden shrines in a single run.
